### Calz

Rust library for multi-calendar conversions. Inspired by the book Calendrical Calculations by Dershowitz & Reingold. It aims to be a scientific reference for calendar arithmetic and conversions.

Repo: [calz](https://github.com/youssefadly237/calz).
